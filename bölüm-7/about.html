Mane Ethical Hairdressing is committed to creating a salon that exists in harmony with our environment.


We pride ourselves on our low-tox environment. Our salon is family friendly so we want to ensure it is a safe and pleasurable place for our clients and their children. ​


We are committed to working with products that align with our values of sustainable and ethical processes. Where we can, we will always support Australian and local businesses.


We promise to take the time to work with you to achieve the hair style you want without compromising your hair integrity and strive to provide you quality service always.


Mane Ethical has been accredited by Sustainable Salons Australia. We have committed ourselves and put in place the processes to ensure every possible measure is taken to create a minimal waste salon.
