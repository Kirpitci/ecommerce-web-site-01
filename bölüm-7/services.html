Free 30 minute consultation.



To make sure we are giving you the service and style you really want we always start with a complimentary 30 minute consultation.


This is where we expertly assess the current health of your hair and forecast any extra measures we need to put in place to ensure you get the results you want.


It gives us a chance to talk about any questions you have and also understand the options of hair products available to find the best one for you.



Our prices listed below are a guide. Please call us or book an appointment online and we will quote accurately after an initial consultation.



When booking a service with Mane Ethical Hairdressing you will be required to pay a non-refundable booking fee that will come off the total cost of your service. The amount of the booking fee will depend on your service and booking method.


Online Bookings


All online bookings will require a booking fee of 20% of the total cost of services booked. You can cancel or change your booking up to 48 hours in advance of your appointment at no cost. If you don’t show up to your appointment, Mane Ethical Hairdressing has the right to charge you the balance of your booking amount left to pay in the salon by way of your online payment method.


Phone and In Person Bookings


booking fee of $25 for all cutting and styling appointments and $50 for colour appointments is required at the time of making your booking. You can pay your booking fee in person using cash or EFT services or over the phone using a credit card. No bookings will be made until you have paid your booking fee. You can cancel or change our booking up to 48 hours in advance of your appointment time at no cost. If you don’t show up to your appointment you will forfeit your booking fee.


Appointment Confirmations


Mane Ethical Hairdressing reserves the right to cancel any appointments that fail to confirm 48 hours out from the appointment time.
